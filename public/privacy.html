1. Introduction

Google Calendar Bulk Edit ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that allows you to select and perform bulk operations on multiple Google Calendar events at once. This Privacy Policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and how we protect your privacy.

By using the Extension, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The Extension stores the following information locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

  • User preferences (highlight color, keyboard shortcuts, UI settings)
  • Authentication state (whether you are signed in)
  • Cached subscription status
  • Undo history for recent bulk operations

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers, except as described below.

2.2 Data Sent to Our Server

When you sign in to the Extension, we collect:

  • Your Google Account email address - Used solely to identify your account for subscription management

This email is sent to our server (gcbulkedit.dev) to:

  • Check your subscription status (free tier vs. paid subscriber)
  • Track how many free bulk operations you have remaining (50 free actions)
  • Process subscription purchases and cancellations

We do not store, transmit, or have access to any of your calendar event data. All calendar operations happen entirely within your browser.

2.3 Google Calendar Data

The Extension accesses your Google Calendar data to perform bulk operations. This includes:

  • Event metadata (titles, times, colors, descriptions)
  • Calendar IDs to identify which calendar events belong to

This data:

  • Is processed entirely within your browser
  • Is never sent to our servers
  • Is never stored, logged, or analyzed by us
  • Is only used to perform actions you explicitly request

The Extension's use of Google Calendar data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes only:

  • Email address: To manage your subscription and track free action usage
  • Local preferences: To remember your settings between sessions
  • Calendar data: To perform bulk move, delete, rename, copy, paste, and color operations that you request

We do not use your data for analytics, tracking, advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the Extension's core functionality.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Stripe: If you subscribe, your email is shared with Stripe to process payments. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing.
  • Google: The Extension uses Google OAuth for authentication and Google Calendar API for event operations. Google's privacy policy applies to these services.

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

  • Email and subscription data: Retained on our server as long as you have an account. You can request deletion by contacting us.
  • Local preferences: Stored until you uninstall the Extension or clear browser data.
  • Calendar data: Never stored - processed in real-time and immediately discarded.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • OAuth 2.0 secure authentication via Google
  • HTTPS encryption for all server communications
  • Secure payment processing through Stripe
  • No storage of sensitive calendar data

7. Permissions Explained

The Extension requests the following permissions:

  • identity: To sign you in with your Google account via OAuth
  • storage: To save your preferences locally in your browser
  • scripting: To inject the Extension into Google Calendar tabs when installed
  • Host permissions (calendar.google.com): To add bulk editing functionality to Google Calendar pages
  • Google Calendar API scope: To read and modify calendar events when you perform bulk operations

We request only the minimum permissions required for the Extension to function.

8. Third-Party Services

The Extension integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google APIs: For authentication and calendar access. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: For payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Revoke access: Remove the Extension's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Settings
  • Unsubscribe: Cancel your subscription at any time through the Extension

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

10. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
