1. Introduction

The Google Calendar Bulk Edit Extension is a browser extension designed to enhance your experience in Google Calendar. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how it is protected.

By using the Extension, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Chrome Extension Data

The Extension may store locally on your device:

  • User preferences or settings (e.g., highlight colors, UI state, toggle options)
  • Temporary selection or UI-related state for the purpose of the extension's functionality

This information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Google Calendar API Data (If Applicable)

If the Extension interacts with your Google Calendar (e.g., reading events, modifying events), it may access:

  • Event metadata (event ID, title, description, start/end times)
  • Calendar metadata (calendar IDs, access level)
  • Your Google Account email address (only to identify the currently active Google Calendar)

This data:

  • Is only used to perform actions you request inside Google Calendar
  • Is not stored on external servers
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Is never used for advertising or profiling

The Extension complies fully with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information accessed by the Extension solely to:

  • Provide functionality such as highlighting, selecting, or modifying events
  • Display UI-enhancing features inside Google Calendar
  • Ensure actions are performed under the correct Google account
  • Provide a smooth and consistent user experience

We do not use your data for analytics, tracking, advertising, or selling to third parties.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not:

  • Sell data
  • Share data with advertising providers
  • Transfer data to third parties
  • Store any personally identifiable information on servers

Your data stays 100% on your local device unless Google transmits it through its own API as part of actions you initiate (e.g., updating an event).

5. Data Security

Although the Extension does not transmit your data to external servers, we still take security seriously. All data accessed through the Extension uses:

  • OAuth 2.0 secure authentication via Google
  • Industry-standard security handled by the Chrome Extensions platform

No external databases or servers are used.

6. Permissions

The Extension may request the following permissions:

  • identity - For authenticating with your Google account
  • activeTab or scripting - To interact with Google Calendar pages
  • storage - To save extension preferences locally
  • Google Calendar API scopes (if applicable) only as required for the Extension's features

We request only the minimal permissions needed for functionality.

7. Third-Party Services

The Extension uses Google APIs. Your use of Google services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

8. Microtransactions (Future Features)

In the future, the Extension may offer optional microtransactions to unlock premium features or additional functionality.

If such features are added, they will only use data necessary to process the transaction, such as payment information handled through trusted third-party payment processors.

Any personal or calendar data will not be used for payment processing.

9. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
